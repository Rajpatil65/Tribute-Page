Tribute to Atal Bihari Vajpayee

1924 - 2018

Atal Bihari Vajpayee was an Indian statesman who served three terms as the Prime Minister of India: first for a term of 13 days in 1996, then for a period of 13 months from 1998 to 1999, and finally, for a full term from 1999 to 2004.

He was a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), and a leader admired across party lines for his statesmanship and oratory skills. Vajpayee ji was known for his eloquence, moderation, and inclusive politics.

During his tenure as Prime Minister, India made significant strides in various fields including infrastructure, education, and foreign policy. He was also a prominent poet, known for his literary works in Hindi.

Atal Bihari Vajpayee passed away on August 16, 2018, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire millions of Indians.

This tribute page is created to honor his contributions to the nation and his lasting impact on Indian politics.
